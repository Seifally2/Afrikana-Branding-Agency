FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

Our web development service includes custom design, responsive development, content management system setup (if required), basic SEO configuration, contact form integration, and one round of revisions. Additional features like e-commerce, custom animations, or advanced functionality would be quoted separately.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks from kickoff to launch. More complex projects like custom web applications or e-commerce sites can take 8-12 weeks or longer. We provide a detailed timeline during our discovery phase after understanding all project requirements.

Yes, we offer several ongoing support options. All projects include 30 days of post-launch support for minor fixes. After that, we offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates, security patches, backups, and a set number of support hours. We can customize a support package based on your specific needs.

We're platform agnostic and work with the best technology for each project. For CMS websites, we commonly use WordPress, Shopify, and custom solutions. For web applications, we work with React, Vue, Laravel, and Node.js. For mobile apps, we use Flutter, React Native, and native iOS/Android development. We'll recommend the best stack for your specific needs.

Communication is key to project success. We use Slack for quick questions, email for formal communication, and Zoom for meetings. Each project has a dedicated project manager who serves as your single point of contact. We provide weekly progress reports and schedule regular check-in meetings based on the project phase and your preferences.
